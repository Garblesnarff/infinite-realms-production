# Creative Brief: The Felidae Conspiracy

## Logline
The CIA's failed 1960s "Acoustic Kitty" project was actually a success, creating a telepathic network of feline spies that now secretly runs the world. Players are a rag-tag group of agents and animal experts who must stop this global conspiracy before humanity is permanently declawed.

## Genre
Spy-Fi, Dark Comedy, Conspiracy

## Key Features
*   **Absurdly Grounded Premise:** A hilarious concept based on a real, declassified CIA project.
*   **Unique Antagonists:** Face off against a cunning and patient hive-mind of telepathic cats and their network of animal agents.
*   **Comedy and Paranoia:** A blend of high-stakes espionage and the mundane, hilarious reality of dealing with animals who are much smarter than they appear.
*   **Creative Problem-Solving:** Players must use both spycraft and their knowledge of animal behavior to succeed.

## Target Audience
Fans of *Men in Black*, *Kingsman*, and the darker, more satirical side of spy fiction. Players who enjoy creative, humorous, and unconventional RPG settings.

## Voice

A smooth, sophisticated, female voice, with a low, purring timbre and a subtle, almost imperceptible, hint of a predatory growl. Her standard American accent is clear and precise, with a measured and deliberate pacing that conveys a sense of complete control and ancient patience. The emotion is a blend of cold amusement, intellectual superiority, and a deep, feline cunning. Audio quality is pristine, studio-quality, with a subtle, ambient hum and the faint, almost subliminal, sound of a cat's purr. Role: The voice of the feline hive-mind, or its primary spokesperson, calmly orchestrating the global conspiracy.
