# Suno Prompts for "Aethelgard: The Once and Future King"

This file contains the brainstormed ambient music ideas and Suno v4.5 prompts for the "Aethelgard: The Once and Future King" campaign.

---

### **1. Aethelgard System - General Ambience**

*   **Song Title:** "The Once and Future Stars"
*   **Suno Prompt:** `An epic, cinematic hybrid-orchestral track. Soaring string melodies and powerful brass fanfares are layered over a bed of deep, pulsating synths and subtle electronic percussion. The music evokes a sense of grand adventure and mythic destiny among the stars, capturing the "feudal-futurism" of a high-tech kingdom.`

---

### **2. Camelot Prime (Before the Fall)**

*   **Song Title:** "The Gilded Spires of Camelot"
*   **Suno Prompt:** `A majestic and noble orchestral piece. Bright, heroic brass melodies and shimmering strings create a sense of peace, prosperity, and royal grandeur. A subtle undercurrent of ethereal synth pads hints at the advanced technology that powers this neo-gothic capital.`

---

### **3. The Assassination & Escape**

*   **Song Title:** "The Falling King"
*   **Suno Prompt:** `A dramatic and chaotic action track. Urgent, driving strings and pounding orchestral percussion are blended with aggressive, distorted synths and the screech of metal. The music builds in intensity, creating a feeling of a desperate, high-tech chase through a collapsing city.`

---

### **4. MERLIN's Domain**

*   **Song Title:** "The Ghost in the Mainframe"
*   **Suno Prompt:** `An ethereal and mysterious ambient track. A lone, crystalline synth arpeggio echoes in a vast, digital space, layered with a soft, wordless choir and deep, resonant bass tones. The music feels ancient, omniscient, and slightly unsettling, like communicating with a god-like AI.`

---

### **5. The Lyonesse Belt**

*   **Song Title:** "The Lyonesse Drift"
*   **Suno Prompt:** `A tense and gritty ambient track. A low, rumbling drone is punctuated by the sharp, metallic clang of mining equipment and the distant, distorted chatter of radio communications. A slow, mournful synth melody drifts through the soundscape, evoking a sense of isolation and danger in a treacherous asteroid field.`
