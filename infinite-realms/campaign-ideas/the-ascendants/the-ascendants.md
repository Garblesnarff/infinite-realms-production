# The Ascendants

*A Superhero / Urban Fantasy campaign — Short Campaign (6–8 sessions) — Medium difficulty*

---

## Campaign Overview

**Title:** The Ascendants
**Campaign Type / Genre:** Superhero / Urban Fantasy
**Estimated Length:** Short Campaign — 6–8 sessions (each session ≈ 3–4 hours)
**Player Level Range:** Start **1** → Finish **5–6**
**Tone (keywords):** heroic, action-packed, modern, consequential, origin-story
**Core Premise (2–4 sentences):**
In the modern-day city of Metroburg, a mysterious event known as the "Ascension" has granted a small percentage of the population incredible, magical powers. The party is a group of these newly-powered individuals, trying to come to terms with their new abilities and their place in a world that is not ready for them. They must decide whether to use their powers for good, for evil, or for their own personal gain, all while dealing with the consequences of their actions and the emergence of other, more powerful and dangerous Ascendants.

---

## What “Medium” Difficulty Means Here

*   **Encounters:** The campaign will feature a mix of classic superhero action, from stopping bank robberies to fighting supervillains, and more personal, character-driven stories about the challenges of living with superpowers.
*   **Public Image:** The party's actions will be reported in the media, and they will have to deal with the court of public opinion. They can become beloved heroes, feared vigilantes, or hated villains.
*   **Consequences:** The emergence of superpowers has thrown the world into chaos. The party's actions will have a direct impact on the future of Metroburg and the fate of the Ascendants.

---

## Story Arc (Short Campaign — 6–8 sessions)

A three-act arc that tells the origin story of a new team of superheroes.

*   **Act I — The Awakening (Sessions 1–2):** The party members discover their new powers and must learn to control them. They have their first encounters with other Ascendants and must decide what they are going to do with their new abilities.
*   **Act II — The Rise of the Supervillain (Sessions 3–5):** A powerful and charismatic Ascendant emerges, using his powers to terrorize the city. The party must team up to stop him, becoming the city's first and only team of superheroes.
*   **Act III — The Secret of the Ascension (Sessions 6–8):** The party has defeated the supervillain, but they have also uncovered a conspiracy that reveals the true, and terrifying, origin of the Ascension. They must make a choice that will determine the future of all Ascendants.

---

## Session-by-Session Outline

### Session 1 — “The Day Everything Changed”

*   **Focus:** Introduction / Origin Story
*   **Key Events:**
    *   Hook: The party members are all ordinary people whose lives are changed forever when they suddenly manifest incredible powers during a city-wide blackout.
    *   The First Test: The party must use their new, uncontrolled powers to save themselves and others from a disaster caused by the blackout (e.g., a collapsing bridge, a hospital losing power).
    *   The First Contact: The party members encounter each other for the first time, realizing that they are not alone.

**Escalation:** The media begins to report on the emergence of super-powered individuals, and the public reaction is a mix of fear and fascination.

### Session 2 — “The Mask”

*   **Focus:** Roleplaying / Character Development
*   **Key Events:**
    *   The New Normal: The party tries to return to their normal lives, but they find that it is impossible. Their powers are a part of them now, and they must learn to live with them.
    *   The Secret Identity: The party must decide whether to reveal their powers to the world or to create secret identities to protect themselves and their loved ones.
    *   The First Team-Up: The party teams up to stop a minor super-powered threat, such as a group of teenagers who are using their new powers to commit petty crimes.

**Escalation:** A charismatic and powerful Ascendant, who calls himself "Apex," appears on the scene, preaching a message of Ascendant superiority.

### Session 3 — “The Rise of Apex”

*   **Focus:** Action / Supervillain
*   **Key Events:**
    *   The New Gang in Town: Apex and his followers, the True Ascendants, begin to consolidate their power in the city's underworld.
    *   The Bank Robbery: The party foils a bank robbery being committed by the True Ascendants, their first real battle against other super-powered individuals.
    *   The Declaration of War: Apex declares war on the city's human authorities and challenges the party to stop him.

**Escalation:** Apex demonstrates the true extent of his power by single-handedly defeating the city's entire police force.

### Session 4 — “The Underground”

*   **Focus:** Investigation / Faction
*   **Key Events:**
    *   The Resistance: The party is contacted by a secret organization of non-powered humans who are trying to fight back against Apex.
    *   The Spy Game: The party must go undercover in Apex's organization to gather information on his plans.
    *   The Super-Weapon: The party discovers that Apex is building a device that will amplify his powers and allow him to control the minds of every person in the city.

**Escalation:** The party's cover is blown, and they must fight their way out of Apex's headquarters.

### Session 5 — “The Battle for Metroburg”

*   **Focus:** Large-Scale Combat / City-Wide Event
*   **Key Events:**
    *   The Attack: Apex launches a full-scale attack on the city.
    *   The Defense of the City: The party must work with the police and the military to defend the city from Apex and his army of True Ascendants.
    *   The Allies: The party is joined by other, independent heroes who have been inspired by their example.

**Escalation:** Apex activates his mind-control device, and the party must fight against their own friends and allies.

### Session 6 — “The Lair of the Villain”

*   **Focus:** Dungeon Crawl / Boss Battle
*   **Key Events:**
    *   The Lair: The party must fight their way through Apex's heavily guarded lair to reach his command center.
    *   The Gauntlet: The party must face off against Apex's most powerful lieutenants.
    *   The Showdown with Apex: The party has a final, epic showdown with Apex.

**Escalation:** Apex, defeated, reveals a shocking secret: the Ascension was not a natural event.

### Session 7 — “The Secret of the Ascension”

*   **Focus:** Revelation / Conspiracy
*   **Key Events:**
    *   The Truth: The party learns that the Ascension was an experiment conducted by a shadowy government agency, Project Chimera, that was trying to create super-soldiers.
    *   The Cover-Up: Project Chimera tries to silence the party and cover up their involvement.
    *   The Evidence: The party must find evidence of Project Chimera's experiments to expose them to the world.

**Escalation:** Project Chimera activates their ultimate weapon: a powerful, unstable Ascendant who they have been keeping in cryo-sleep.

### Session 8 — “The Future of the Ascendants”

*   **Focus:** Climax / Moral Choice
*   **Key Events:**
    *   The Final Battle: The party has a final battle with Project Chimera's ultimate weapon.
    *   The Choice: The party has the evidence they need to expose Project Chimera, but doing so could cause a world-wide panic and lead to the persecution of all Ascendants. They must decide whether to release the information or to keep it a secret.
    *   The Birth of a Team: The party, now a fully-fledged team of superheroes, must decide what their role will be in this new and dangerous world.

## Major NPCs

1.  **Apex** — *The Supervillain*
    *   **Role:** The charismatic and powerful leader of the True Ascendants.
    *   **Personality:** Arrogant, messianic, and believes that the Ascendants are the next stage of human evolution.
    *   **Secret/Flaw:** Apex was a victim of Project Chimera's experiments, and his powers are slowly killing him. His crusade is a desperate attempt to find a cure.

2.  **General Miller** — *The Government Agent*
    *   **Role:** The ruthless and pragmatic leader of Project Chimera.
    *   **Personality:** Cold, calculating, and will do whatever it takes to protect his country.
    *   **Secret/Flaw:** General Miller's son is an Ascendant, a secret that he is desperate to keep.

3.  **Jessica Jones** — *The Reporter*
    *   **Role:** A tenacious and idealistic reporter who is investigating the Ascension.
    *   **Personality:** Smart, brave, and has a deep-seated belief in the power of the truth.
    *   **Secret/Flaw:** Jessica's investigation is not just professional. Her younger brother was one of the first to Ascend, and he has gone missing.

---

## Key Locations

1.  **Metroburg:** A sprawling, modern metropolis that is the epicenter of the Ascension.
2.  **The Rooftops:** The domain of the city's heroes and villains, a place of secret meetings and dramatic battles.
3.  **The Underground:** A hidden world of secret bases, black markets, and underground fight clubs for Ascendants.
4.  **Project Chimera:** A shadowy government facility where the Ascension was born.

---

## Central Conflict

The central conflict is the struggle to define what it means to be a hero in a world that is not ready for them. It is a story about power, responsibility, and the question of whether great power can ever be used for good. The party must not only fight supervillains, but also fight for their place in a world that both fears and needs them.

---

## Unique Mechanics

### 1. **The Power System**

*   The DM can create a unique system for the party's superpowers, allowing them to choose from a variety of powers and to upgrade them as they level up. The powers should be flexible and allow for creative problem-solving.

### 2. **The Public Image**

*   The party's actions will be reported in the media, and they will have a public image that can be positive, negative, or neutral. A positive public image can provide benefits, such as the cooperation of the police and the support of the public. A negative public image can lead to new enemies and obstacles.

### 3. **The Secret Identity**

*   The party can choose to create secret identities to protect themselves and their loved ones. This can lead to a variety of interesting roleplaying opportunities and challenges, as the party must balance their normal lives with their lives as superheroes.
