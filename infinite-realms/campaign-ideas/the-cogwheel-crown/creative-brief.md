# Creative Brief: The Cogwheel Crown

This document outlines the core creative direction for the assets of this campaign, ensuring a consistent and fitting tone across all visuals, audio, and more.

## Art Style: Magical Industrial Realism (Final Fantasy-inspired)

Inspired by the grand, detailed, and technologically advanced fantasy worlds of *Final Fantasy VII Remake* and *Final Fantasy XIV*. This style aims to capture the scheming, innovative, gritty, and hopeful tone of "The Cogwheel Crown," where a magical industrial revolution is reshaping society and powerful factions vie for control.

### Core Principles for Prompts:

*   **Emphasize Detail and Intricacy:** Describe both magical effects and mechanical components with high fidelity, showcasing their complexity and beauty.
*   **Dynamic Energy:** Convey the constant motion and power of the Aether-Crystals, steam, and magical spells through vibrant lighting and particle effects.
*   **Contrast Social Strata:** Visually distinguish between the opulent noble districts, the bustling merchant hubs, and the gritty, working-class factory zones.

---

### Detailed Style Guide for Image Generation:

**1. Overall Vibe & Mood:**
*   **Keywords:** Scheming, innovative, gritty, hopeful, grand, intricate, magical, industrial, dynamic, political.
*   **Feeling:** A world on the cusp of radical change, filled with both immense opportunity and profound danger. The atmosphere should be one of constant tension and underlying power, with moments of breathtaking innovation and desperate struggle.

**2. Line Work & Form:**
*   **Style:** Highly detailed and realistic, with a focus on sharp, precise lines for mechanical elements and flowing, ethereal lines for magical effects. Forms are grounded in realism but can be stylized for dramatic impact.
*   **Textures:** Rich, varied textures for metal (polished, rusted, brass), stone (ancient, new), fabric (luxurious, worn), and the glowing, crystalline structure of Aether-Crystals.
*   **Prompt Example:** "A highly detailed digital painting in the style of Final Fantasy XIV. The focus is on a massive Aether-Crystal conduit, its intricate brass piping glowing with internal magical energy, surrounded by the grimy, detailed machinery of a factory floor."

**3. Color Palette:**
*   **Style:** Rich and varied, blending the earthy tones of traditional fantasy with the metallic and vibrant hues of industrial and magical elements. Dominant colors include deep blues, greens, browns, and grays for the city, accented by the luminous glow of Aether-Crystals (often blue, green, or purple) and magical spells.
*   **Atmospheric Effects:** Smog, steam, and magical particles can diffuse light and create atmospheric depth, especially in factory districts.
*   **Prompt Example:** "...a rich color palette of industrial grays and deep blues, dramatically illuminated by the vibrant, pulsating glow of Aether-Crystals and the fiery orange of a forge, with steam rising into the hazy air."

**4. Character Design:**
*   **Style:** Characters are realistically proportioned with expressive faces. Clothing blends traditional fantasy attire (robes, armor) with steampunk elements (goggles, gears, modified weapons) and modern industrial wear. 
*   **Automatons:** Designs are intricate and functional, often with glowing Aether-Crystal cores, appearing as powerful and elegant mechanical beings.
*   **Expression:** Faces are often determined, cunning, or weary, reflecting the political and social struggles of the city.
*   **Prompt Example:** "A cunning merchant guild leader in a finely tailored suit with subtle clockwork augmentations, his face illuminated by the glowing Aether-Crystal in his hand. His expression is sharp and calculating."

**5. Backgrounds & Environments:**
*   **Style:** Veridia is a city of stark contrasts. The Gilded Spire features opulent, ancient architecture with traditional magical elements. The Foundry District is a sprawling, smoke-choked industrial zone with massive factories and grimy worker housing. The Sky-Docks are bustling hubs of airships and innovation. 
*   **Atmosphere:** Can be grand and awe-inspiring in noble districts, or gritty and oppressive in factory zones. The constant hum of machinery and the crackle of magic are pervasive.
*   **Details:** Exposed pipes, massive gears, steam vents, glowing Aether-Crystal conduits, airship docks, and the subtle presence of both ancient magical runes and modern industrial designs.
*   **Prompt Example:** "A panoramic view of the Foundry District in Veridia. Towering, smoke-belching factories with intricate piping and massive gears dominate the landscape, their Aether-Crystal cores glowing with internal energy. Airships dock at distant Sky-Docks, silhouetted against a smoggy sky."

**6. Shading & Lighting:**
*   **Style:** Dynamic and often dramatic. Light sources are varied: the glow of Aether-Crystals, the fire of forges, the steam from vents, and the ambient light of the city. 
*   **Shadows:** Used to define form, create depth, and add drama. Shadows can be sharp and angular in industrial areas, or soft and ethereal around magical effects.
*   **Purpose:** Lighting is used to highlight innovation, emphasize power, and create a sense of tension and intrigue. It makes the magical industrial revolution feel tangible and impactful.
*   **Prompt Example:** "...dramatically lit by the pulsating blue glow of a massive Aether-Crystal, casting long, sharp shadows of the factory machinery across the floor. Steam rises into the air, diffused by the light, creating a sense of immense power."

**7. Composition:**
*   **Style:** Often uses wide, cinematic shots to capture the grandeur and scale of the city and its industrial complexes. Can also use tight, action-oriented shots for combat or moments of intense magical/technological interaction.
*   **Techniques:** Employ leading lines (e.g., pipes, airship paths, energy conduits) to guide the eye. Frame characters against vast industrial backdrops or glowing Aether-Crystals. Emphasize verticality and depth in cityscapes. Dynamic angles can highlight action and power.
*   **Prompt Example:** "A wide, cinematic shot of a massive airship, its engines glowing with Aether-Crystal energy, soaring over the Foundry District. The composition emphasizes the scale of the industrial revolution, with towering factories and glowing conduits below."

## Voice

A sharp, intelligent, female voice, clear and crisp, with a hint of metallic resonance, like the precise ticking of a masterfully crafted clockwork device. Her standard American accent is well-enunciated and commanding, with a confident, measured pacing that conveys a sense of purpose and a clear, driving rhythm. The emotion is a blend of ambitious energy, intellectual curiosity, and a subtle, almost ruthless pragmatism. Audio quality is pristine, studio-quality, with the faint, ambient hum of whirring gears and the distant, rhythmic clang of industry. Role: The brilliant inventor at the heart of the magical industrial revolution, or a political leader navigating the treacherous currents of change.
