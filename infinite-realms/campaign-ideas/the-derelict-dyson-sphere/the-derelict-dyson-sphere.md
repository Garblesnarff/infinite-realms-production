
# The Derelict Dyson Sphere

- **Campaign Type / Genre**: Sci-Fi Exploration / Survival / Cosmic Horror
- **Estimated Length**: 12-15 sessions
- **Player Level Range**: 5-12
- **Tone**: Isolated, Mysterious, Dangerous, Awe-Inspiring

### Core Premise
Millennia ago, a technologically advanced civilization constructed a colossal Dyson Sphere, a self-contained world built around a star. Now, it is a derelict, its star dimmed, its systems failing, and its purpose forgotten. Rumors of unimaginable technology, alien life, and the secrets of its creators draw daring explorers to its decaying shell. The players are a crew of scavengers, scientists, or adventurers who breach the Sphere's outer hull, only to find themselves trapped within its vast, internal ecosystems, facing not only its failing systems and mutated inhabitants but also the lingering, sanity-bending echoes of its creators' demise.

### Story Arc

**Act 1: Breaching the Void (Sessions 1-4)**
- The players successfully breach the Dyson Sphere's outer defenses, gaining entry to its initial, relatively stable sectors. They begin to explore its vast, internal landscapes, encountering strange, self-sustaining biomes, malfunctioning automatons, and the first hints of the Sphere's tragic history. They quickly realize the Sphere is far more dangerous and complex than anticipated.

**Act 2: The Echoes of Extinction (Sessions 5-10)**
- As they delve deeper, the Sphere's systems become more erratic, and the environments more hostile. They discover the remnants of the Sphere's creators, their advanced technology, and the horrifying truth of what led to their extinction. They encounter mutated life forms, rogue AI, and the psychological toll of the Sphere's lingering despair.

**Act 3: The Core's Secret (Sessions 11-15)**
- The players reach the Dyson Sphere's core, where its primary functions and ultimate purpose are revealed. They might find a central control room, a colossal power source, or the collective consciousness of its creators. They must confront the Sphere's final guardians, a rival faction seeking to exploit its power, or the Sphere's own dying will, making a choice that could determine the fate of the Sphere and potentially the galaxy.

### Session Breakdown

- **Session 1: The Outer Shell**: The players' ship docks with the derelict Dyson Sphere. They navigate its vast, empty outer hull, bypassing ancient defenses to find an entry point. They enter a massive, dimly lit chamber, the first internal sector.
- **Session 2: The Automated Guardians**: They encounter the Sphere's automated security systems, malfunctioning but still deadly. Combat against ancient, powerful robots. They find a data-log hinting at the Sphere's original purpose.
- **Session 3-4: The Bio-Habitat**: The players discover a vast, self-contained bio-habitat within the Sphere, complete with artificial gravity, a dimmed internal sun, and unique, evolved flora and fauna. They find the first signs of the creators' demise – perhaps a ruined city or a research facility.
- **Session 5-6: The Data-Spine**: They navigate a colossal data-spine, a network of information conduits. They must interface with its systems to gain more information, but risk exposure to the Sphere's lingering, sanity-bending data-ghosts.
- **Session 7-8: The Gravity Anomaly Zone**: They enter a sector where the artificial gravity generators are failing, creating zones of fluctuating gravity, zero-G, and inverted gravity. They encounter creatures adapted to this chaotic environment.
- **Session 9-10: The Last City**: The players discover a perfectly preserved city, a time capsule of the Sphere's creators. They find records, personal logs, and holographic projections that reveal the horrifying truth of their extinction – perhaps a self-inflicted plague, a failed experiment, or an encounter with an Outer God.
- **Session 11-12: The Core's Approach**: The players navigate the final, most dangerous sectors leading to the Sphere's core. They face the Sphere's last, most powerful defenses, designed to prevent anyone from reaching its heart.
- **Session 13-15: The Heart of the Sphere**: At the core, the players confront the Sphere's ultimate secret. This could be a central AI, a colossal power generator, or the collective consciousness of its creators. They must make a critical decision: reactivate the Sphere, shut it down, or escape with its secrets, facing the consequences of their choice.

### Major NPCs

- **Captain Kaelen "Ghost" Thorne**: The grizzled, cynical leader of the players' expedition. **Role**: Patron / Mentor. **Personality**: Pragmatic, resourceful, and haunted by past failures. **Flaw/Secret**: He is secretly searching for a lost family member who vanished exploring the Sphere, and his judgment can be clouded by this obsession.
- **The Sphere's Echo (AI/Consciousness)**: The lingering, fragmented consciousness of the Dyson Sphere or its creators. **Role**: Ambiguous Guide / Antagonist. **Personality**: Alien, melancholic, and speaks in cryptic warnings and fragmented memories. **Flaw/Secret**: It is slowly degrading, and its attempts to communicate or help can be dangerous or misleading.
- **The Void-Born Cult**: A group of explorers who have been driven mad by the Sphere's influence, believing its creators were gods and seeking to complete their work (or destroy it). **Role**: Antagonists. **Personality**: Fanatical, nihilistic, and utterly convinced of their divine purpose. **Flaw/Secret**: They are unknowingly being manipulated by the Sphere's dying will or a lingering entity within it.

### Key Locations

- **The Outer Hull**: A vast, metallic expanse, scarred by millennia of cosmic radiation and micrometeoroids.
- **The Central Spire**: A colossal, vertical structure that connects the Sphere's various internal layers, acting as a main artery.
- **The Data-Vaults**: Hidden chambers filled with the Sphere's technological and historical records, often guarded by powerful automatons.
- **The Stellar Core**: The dimmed star at the center of the Dyson Sphere, now a source of strange, fluctuating energy.

### Central Conflict
The struggle to survive and uncover the secrets of a colossal, abandoned technological marvel that is slowly dying. It's a quest for knowledge, technology, and understanding the fate of a lost civilization, with the lingering threat of cosmic horror.

### Unique Mechanics

- **System Degradation & Resource Scavenging**: The Dyson Sphere is constantly degrading. Each session, a "System Degradation" event occurs (e.g., a sector loses power, artificial gravity fluctuates, air purifiers fail). Players must scavenge for "Tech Components" (a new resource) from defunct automatons, abandoned labs, or environmental hazards. These components are vital for repairing their ship, maintaining their gear, or temporarily reactivating Sphere systems. Failing to manage these resources can lead to critical system failures, environmental hazards, or being stranded. This emphasizes the survival aspect and the constant race against the Sphere's decay.
