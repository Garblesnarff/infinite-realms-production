# The Gourmet Gauntlet

*A Cooking Competition / Slice of Life campaign — Short Campaign (6–8 sessions) — Light difficulty*

---

## Campaign Overview

**Title:** The Gourmet Gauntlet
**Campaign Type / Genre:** Cooking Competition / Slice of Life
**Estimated Length:** Short Campaign — 6–8 sessions (each session ≈ 3–4 hours)
**Player Level Range:** Start **1** → Finish **5–6**
**Tone (keywords):** flavorful, competitive, charming, low-stakes, heartwarming
**Core Premise (2–4 sentences):**
The party is a team of aspiring chefs, competing in the world-renowned Gourmet Gauntlet, a culinary competition that will take them across the land in search of rare ingredients and exotic recipes. They must hone their skills, impress the discerning judges, and out-cook a cast of eccentric rival chefs. The campaign is a lighthearted and flavorful adventure about the joy of food, the thrill of competition, and the bonds of friendship forged in the heat of the kitchen.

---

## What “Light” Difficulty Means Here

*   **Encounters:** The challenges in this campaign are culinary, not combative. The party will face off against rival chefs in a series of cooking challenges, and they will need to use their creativity, their knowledge of ingredients, and their teamwork to win.
*   **Exploration:** The party will travel to a variety of charming and picturesque locations in search of rare ingredients. This is an opportunity for roleplaying, world-building, and lighthearted adventure.
*   **Consequences:** The stakes are low, but the emotions are high. The worst that can happen is that the party loses a cooking challenge or gets a bad review from a food critic. The goal is to tell a charming and satisfying story about the pursuit of culinary excellence.

---

## Story Arc (Short Campaign — 6–8 sessions)

A three-act arc that follows the party's journey through the Gourmet Gauntlet competition.

*   **Act I — The Appetizer (Sessions 1–2):** The party enters the Gourmet Gauntlet and competes in the preliminary rounds. They get to know their rivals and the judges, and they get their first taste of the high-stakes world of competitive cooking.
*   **Act II — The Main Course (Sessions 3–5):** The competition heats up. The party must travel to different regions to learn new cooking techniques and find rare ingredients. Each session is a new culinary challenge in a new location.
*   **Act III — The Dessert (Sessions 6–8):** The party has made it to the final round of the Gourmet Gauntlet. They must create a three-course meal that will impress the judges and win them the coveted title of Culinary Champions.

## Session-by-Session Outline

### Session 1 — “The Qualifying Round”

*   **Focus:** Introduction / Skill Challenge
*   **Key Events:**
    *   Hook: The party, a team of aspiring chefs, enters the qualifying round of the Gourmet Gauntlet.
    *   The Signature Dish: The party must create a signature dish that represents their team's culinary philosophy.
    *   The Judges: The party presents their dish to the three judges: a stern and traditionalist master chef, a flamboyant and avant-garde food critic, and a cheerful and folksy grandmother who just loves good food.

**Escalation:** The party's main rivals, a team of arrogant and technically brilliant chefs, try to sabotage their dish.

### Session 2 — “The Mystery Box”

*   **Focus:** Cooking Challenge / Improvisation
*   **Key Events:**
    *   The First Round: The first official round of the Gourmet Gauntlet begins.
    *   The Mystery Box: The party is given a box of strange and exotic ingredients and must create a dish on the spot.
    *   The First Elimination: One of the rival teams is eliminated from the competition.

**Escalation:** The mystery ingredients include a live, and very grumpy, flumph.

### Session 3 — “The Mountain's Bounty”

*   **Focus:** Exploration / Foraging
*   **Key Events:**
    *   The Next Challenge: For the next challenge, the teams must travel to the mountains and create a dish using only ingredients that they can forage themselves.
    *   The Foraging Expedition: The party must use their knowledge of nature and their survival skills to find edible plants, mushrooms, and game.
    *   The Mountain King: The party encounters the king of the mountain, a friendly but territorial galeb duhr who will only allow them to take ingredients if they can impress him with a delicious meal.

**Escalation:** A blizzard hits the mountain, and the party must find shelter and cook their dish in a makeshift kitchen.

### Session 4 — “The Coastal Kitchen”

*   **Focus:** Travel / Fishing
*   **Key Events:**
    *   The Seaside Challenge: The next challenge takes place in a quaint coastal village. The teams must create a dish using fresh seafood.
    *   The Fishing Trip: The party must go out on a fishing boat and catch their own seafood.
    *   The Kraken: The party accidentally hooks a baby kraken, and they must find a way to unhook it without angering its mother.

**Escalation:** The party's rivals try to steal their prize-winning catch.

### Session 5 — “The Urban Eatery”

*   **Focus:** Social / Street Food
*   **Key Events:**
    *   The City Challenge: The next challenge takes place in a bustling metropolis. The teams must create a dish that represents the city's diverse and vibrant street food culture.
    *   The Food Tour: The party goes on a food tour of the city, sampling the local cuisine and looking for inspiration.
    *   The Pop-Up Restaurant: The party must open a pop-up restaurant for one night and sell their dish to the public.

**Escalation:** The city's most notorious food critic, a vampire with a very refined palate, shows up at their pop-up.

### Session 6 — “The Semi-Finals”

*   **Focus:** High-Stakes Cooking / Drama
*   **Key Events:**
    *   The Semi-Finals: The party has made it to the semi-finals of the Gourmet Gauntlet.
    *   The Dueling Dishes: The party must compete in a head-to-head cooking duel against their main rivals.
    *   The Secret Ingredient: The secret ingredient for the duel is a rare and magical flower that changes its flavor depending on the emotions of the person who is cooking it.

**Escalation:** The party's rivals try to use emotional manipulation to sabotage their dish.

### Session 7 — “The Grand Finale”

*   **Focus:** Climax / Epic Meal
*   **Key Events:**
    *   The Final Round: The party has made it to the final round of the Gourmet Gauntlet.
    *   The Three-Course Meal: The party must create a three-course meal: an appetizer, a main course, and a dessert.
    *   The Performance: The final round is not just about the food; it is about the performance. The party must present their meal with flair and showmanship.

**Escalation:** The party's oven breaks down in the middle of the final round, and they must find a creative way to finish their dish.

### Session 8 — “The Culinary Champions”

*   **Focus:** Resolution / Epilogue
*   **Key Events:**
    *   The Winner: The judges announce the winner of the Gourmet Gauntlet.
    *   The Celebration: The party celebrates their victory (or commiserates their loss) with their friends, family, and rivals.
    *   The Future: The party must decide what to do next. Do they open their own restaurant, write a cookbook, or travel the world in search of new culinary adventures?

## Major NPCs

1.  **Chef Antoine** — *The Head Judge*
    *   **Role:** The stern and traditionalist head judge of the Gourmet Gauntlet.
    *   **Personality:** A culinary perfectionist with impossibly high standards.
    *   **Secret/Flaw:** Chef Antoine is secretly a fan of simple, rustic cuisine, but he feels that he has to maintain his image as a gourmet snob.

2.  **Fifi Flambé** — *The Flamboyant Judge*
    *   **Role:** The avant-garde food critic and judge.
    *   **Personality:** Flamboyant, eccentric, and always looking for the next new thing in cuisine.
    *   **Secret/Flaw:** Fifi has a notoriously weak stomach and is secretly terrified of trying new foods.

3.  **Grandma Mildred** — *The Folksy Judge*
    *   **Role:** The third judge, a cheerful and kind-hearted grandmother.
    *   **Personality:** Warm, nurturing, and believes that the most important ingredient is love.
    *   **Secret/Flaw:** Grandma Mildred is a powerful and ancient gold dragon in disguise.

## Key Locations

1.  **The Gourmet Gauntlet Arena:** A massive, state-of-the-art kitchen stadium where the cooking competition is held.
2.  **The Whispering Mountains:** A range of mountains that are home to a wide variety of rare and magical ingredients.
3.  **The Azure Coast:** A beautiful coastline with a rich and diverse marine ecosystem.
4.  **The City of a Thousand Flavors:** A bustling metropolis with a vibrant and diverse street food culture.

---

## Central Conflict

The central conflict is the pursuit of culinary excellence. It is a story about the passion, creativity, and dedication that it takes to become a great chef. The party must not only master the technical skills of cooking, but also learn to cook with heart, soul, and a little bit of magic.

## Unique Mechanics

### 1. **The Cooking System**

*   The cooking challenges can be run as a series of skill challenges, with the party making checks in skills like Nature (for foraging), Sleight of Hand (for chopping and plating), and Performance (for presentation). The DM can also create a simple system for tracking the quality of a dish, which can be affected by the ingredients used, the techniques employed, and the creativity of the chefs.

### 2. **The Recipe Book**

*   The party will collect recipes over the course of the campaign. These recipes can provide bonuses in cooking challenges, and they can also be used to create delicious and magical meals that can provide temporary buffs to the party.

### 3. **The Flavor Profile**

*   Each dish has a flavor profile, which can be represented by a set of keywords (e.g., sweet, savory, spicy, magical). The judges will have their own preferences, and the party will need to tailor their dishes to the judges' tastes to win.
