
# The Somnium Heist

- **Campaign Type / Genre**: Psychedelic Fantasy / Heist / Dream Exploration
- **Estimated Length**: 8-10 sessions
- **Player Level Range**: 7-12
- **Tone**: Surreal, Mind-bending, Thrilling, Philosophical

### Core Premise
A slumbering god, known only as the "Dreamer," is the source of all magic in the world. For centuries, its dreams have shaped reality, but now, a fragment of its divine essence, a powerful artifact known as the "Spark of Creation," has become trapped within its deepest, most chaotic dreamscape. A cabal of desperate mages and scholars believes that retrieving this Spark is the only way to prevent the Dreamer's impending awakening, which would unravel reality itself. The players are a specialized team, capable of navigating the perilous and ever-shifting landscapes of the Dreamer's mind, tasked with pulling off the ultimate heist: stealing from a god's subconscious.

### Story Arc

**Act 1: The Lucid Gateway (Sessions 1-3)**
- The players are recruited by the cabal and undergo rigorous training to enter the Dreamer's mind. Their first missions involve navigating the outer layers of the dreamscape, learning its rules, and gathering fragments of the Dreamer's memories to locate the Spark. They encounter dream-entities and the Dreamer's subconscious defenses.

**Act 2: The Nightmare Labyrinth (Sessions 4-7)**
- The team delves deeper into the Dreamer's mind, where its fears, anxieties, and forgotten traumas manifest as dangerous environments and powerful guardians. They must overcome psychological challenges, solve surreal puzzles, and outwit the Dreamer's own defenses, which grow increasingly hostile as they approach the Spark.

**Act 3: The Awakening (Sessions 8-10)**
- The players reach the core of the dreamscape where the Spark resides, but their presence begins to stir the Dreamer. Reality outside starts to fray. They must execute a complex plan to extract the Spark, facing the full might of the Dreamer's subconscious, and escape before the god fully awakens and consumes them all.

### Session Breakdown

- **Session 1: Recruitment & Training**: The players are brought together by the cabal. They undergo initial dream-walking exercises, learning to manipulate minor aspects of the dreamscape and encountering their first dream-entities.
- **Session 2: The Memory Palace**: Their first mission into the Dreamer's mind: navigating a vast, shifting library of its memories to find clues about the Spark's location. They must avoid "memory echoes" – powerful, replayed moments from the Dreamer's past.
- **Session 3: The Dreamer's Defenses**: The players encounter the first true guardians of the dreamscape – constructs of the Dreamer's subconscious designed to repel intruders. They must find a way to bypass or defeat them.
- **Session 4-5: The City of Fears**: The team enters a section of the dreamscape where the Dreamer's anxieties manifest as a sprawling, oppressive city. They must navigate its ever-changing streets and overcome personal fears that are projected onto them.
- **Session 6-7: The Trauma Vault**: Deeper still, they find a vault where the Dreamer's forgotten traumas are locked away. The Spark is nearby, but the vault is guarded by a powerful, distorted manifestation of the Dreamer's pain.
- **Session 8: The Plan**: With the Spark's location confirmed, the team must devise a detailed heist plan, considering the dreamscape's unique physics and the Dreamer's defenses.
- **Session 9-10: The Grand Heist & Escape**: The execution of the plan. This is a multi-stage, high-stakes sequence involving stealth, combat, and reality-bending puzzles. As they attempt to extract the Spark, the Dreamer begins to awaken, causing the dreamscape to collapse around them. They must escape before being trapped forever.

### Major NPCs

- **Master Elara Vane**: The leader of the cabal. **Role**: Patron / Guide. **Personality**: Brilliant, driven, but haunted by the weight of her responsibility. She believes this is the only way to save reality. **Flaw/Secret**: She has been subtly influenced by the Dreamer's subconscious, causing her to occasionally give contradictory or unsettling advice.
- **The Sandman**: A mysterious, neutral entity that patrols the dreamscape, maintaining a fragile order. **Role**: Obstacle / Informant. **Personality**: Enigmatic, speaks in riddles, and is bound by ancient laws of the dream. **Flaw/Secret**: The Sandman is a fragment of the Dreamer's own consciousness, unaware of its true nature, and its actions are ultimately to protect the Dreamer, even from the players.
- **The Nightmare Weaver**: A powerful dream-entity that feeds on the Dreamer's negative emotions. **Role**: Antagonist. **Personality**: Sadistic, manipulative, and revels in chaos. It sees the players as a new source of delicious fear. **Flaw/Secret**: It is bound to the Dreamer's deepest fears and cannot leave the dreamscape, making it a formidable but contained threat.

### Key Locations

- **The Lucid Gateway**: The cabal's hidden sanctum, a place where reality and dream blur.
- **The Shifting Spires**: The outer layers of the Dreamer's mind, a landscape of impossible architecture and floating islands.
- **The Whispering Woods**: A forest where trees whisper forgotten secrets and paths constantly change.
- **The Obsidian Heart**: The deepest part of the dreamscape, where the Spark resides, a place of raw, unfiltered divine power.

### Central Conflict
The central conflict is a race against time to prevent the collapse of reality by performing a daring heist within the mind of a slumbering god. It's a battle against the subconscious, against the very fabric of existence, and against the players' own perceptions.

### Unique Mechanics

- **Dream Logic & Reality Bending**: The rules of reality are fluid within the Dreamer's mind. Players can attempt to manipulate the dreamscape using their imagination and Charisma (Persuasion/Deception) checks. For example, a player might try to convince a dream-guard that they are its long-lost sibling, or attempt to manifest a key by truly believing it exists in their pocket. Critical successes can lead to powerful, temporary alterations of the environment or dream-entities, while critical failures can cause the dreamscape to turn hostile or inflict psychic damage. This encourages creative problem-solving and roleplaying over brute force.
