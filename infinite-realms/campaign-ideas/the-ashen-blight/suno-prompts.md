# Suno Prompts for "The Ashen Blight"

This file contains the brainstormed ambient music ideas and Suno v4.5 prompts for the "The Ashen Blight" campaign.

---

### **1. The Blighted Lands - General Ambience**

*   **Song Title:** "The Ashen Lament"
*   **Suno Prompt:** `A desolate and oppressive dark ambient track. A low, guttural drone is layered with the sound of a cold, howling wind and the distant, mournful cry of a lone wolf. A sparse, detuned piano melody plays a simple, repeating phrase, creating a sense of unending decay and hopelessness in a world consumed by a supernatural plague.`

---

### **2. Oakhaven, the Dying Hamlet**

*   **Song Title:** "The Last Hearth"
*   **Suno Prompt:** `A melancholic and gritty ambient piece. The sound of a crackling fire and the soft, rhythmic creak of a wooden sign are the only signs of life. A lone, sorrowful cello plays a simple, folk-like melody, while a subtle, underlying synth pad adds a sense of creeping dread and isolation.`

---

### **3. The Weeping Wood**

*   **Song Title:** "The Forest of Weeping Sores"
*   **Suno Prompt:** `A tense and unsettling soundscape. The track is built on a bed of wet, squelching sounds and the snapping of twigs, layered with the faint, high-pitched whine of insects. A slow, dissonant string melody, full of microtonal shifts, creates a sense of a forest that is actively hostile and unnatural.`

---

### **4. Greyport, the Decaying City**

*   **Song Title:** "The Rain-Slicked Stones of Greyport"
*   **Suno Prompt:** `A grim and atmospheric city soundscape. The constant sound of a cold, steady rain is mixed with the distant, echoing clang of a blacksmith's hammer and the mournful toll of a cathedral bell. A low, oppressive synth drone underpins the track, creating a sense of urban decay and moral rot.`

---

### **5. The Baron's Masquerade**

*   **Song Title:** "The Danse Macabre"
*   **Suno Prompt:** `A dark and elegant classical piece. A string quartet plays a beautiful, yet unsettling, waltz in a minor key. The music is punctuated by the faint, almost imperceptible sound of a heart beating, which grows slowly louder and more erratic as the track progresses, hinting at the corruption hidden beneath the opulent facade.`

---

### **6. The Sunken Temple of Nalthur**

*   **Song Title:** "The Drowned God's Slumber"
*   **Suno Prompt:** `A claustrophobic and aquatic dark ambient track. The sound of muffled, underwater movement and the slow, rhythmic dripping of water are layered with a deep, resonant, and distorted choral chant. A sense of immense pressure and ancient, slumbering evil pervades the track.`

---

### **7. The Ashen Disciples**

*   **Song Title:** "The Hymn of the Blight"
*   **Suno Prompt:** `A ritualistic and menacing track. A low, guttural chant in a fictional, blasphemous language is layered with the sound of scraping stone and the rhythmic pounding of a large, primitive drum. A high, piercing synth note, like a nerve being scraped, builds in intensity throughout the track, creating a sense of impending doom.`

---

### **8. The Final Confrontation**

*   **Song Title:** "The Heart of the Blight"
*   **Suno Prompt:** `An epic and terrifying orchestral-industrial hybrid. Thunderous, distorted drums and screeching, industrial noise are blended with a full, screaming choir and a powerful, dissonant brass section. The music is a chaotic and overwhelming assault on the senses, representing a battle against a cosmic, world-ending threat.`
