**Tides of Fortune: The Pirate King's Legacy**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Directive:** This is the foundational context. All generated content must align with this core premise.
*   **Content:** The players, a ragtag crew of sailors, outcasts, and dreamers, acquire their own ship and set sail across the treacherous Sapphire Archipelago in search of the legendary Treasure of the Pirate King. As they navigate rival pirate crews, ancient sea monsters, and cursed waters, they discover that the real treasure isn't gold and jewelsâ€”it's the freedom of the open sea and the bonds forged in the face of adversity. But when a ruthless pirate lord learns of their quest, the players must choose between the easy path of piracy or the honorable path of adventure, all while racing against time to claim the greatest treasure the ocean has ever known.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Directive:** The Lore Generation Agent must create detailed entries for each of the following prompts. These entries will form the historical and cultural bedrock of the world.
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the founding of the Sapphire Archipelago and the rise of the Pirate King who unified the pirate fleets centuries ago.
    *   Write the history of the Pirate King's legendary treasure and the curse that protects it from unworthy claimants.
    *   Describe the various pirate crews and factions that operate in the archipelago and their complex relationships.
    *   Explain the nature of the sea monsters and supernatural phenomena that guard the treasure's location.
    *   Detail the port towns and hidden coves that serve as pirate havens and information hubs.
    *   Write about the "Pirate's Code" - the unwritten rules that govern pirate society and treasure hunting.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Directive:** The Faction Generation Agent must create a detailed profile for each faction listed below. Each profile must contain the specified fields.
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **The Black Fleet** (Major)
        *   **Goals:** To claim the Pirate King's treasure and use it to establish dominance over all pirate crews in the archipelago.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Military structure led by Captain Blackthorn Reyes, with loyal captains commanding individual ships.
        *   **Public Agenda:** We are the true inheritors of the Pirate King's legacy and will claim what is rightfully ours.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** To use the treasure's power to create a pirate empire that dominates legitimate trade routes.
        *   **Assets:** Largest pirate fleet, undead ghost ships, network of spies in every port.
        *   **Relationships:** Antagonistic toward all other pirate crews; willing to form temporary alliances against greater threats.
    *   **The Free Brothers** (Major)
        *   **Goals:** To protect the freedom of the seas and prevent any one power from dominating the archipelago.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Democratic council of ship captains who vote on major decisions.
        *   **Public Agenda:** Every sailor deserves freedom and a fair share of any treasure found.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** To discover the treasure's location first and use it to fund a pirate utopia.
        *   **Assets:** Network of allied ships, hidden supply caches, reputation for fairness.
        *   **Relationships:** Friendly toward independent crews; hostile toward the Black Fleet and overly aggressive pirates.
    *   **The Merchant's Guild** (Minor)
        *   **Goals:** To establish safe trade routes through the archipelago and eliminate pirate threats to commerce.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Council of wealthy merchants with ties to legitimate governments.
        *   **Public Agenda:** We bring civilization and honest trade to these lawless waters.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** To acquire the Pirate King's treasure and use it to fund their own private navy.
        *   **Assets:** Significant wealth, political influence, bounties on pirate heads.
        *   **Relationships:** Hostile toward all pirate factions; may hire players for "special operations."

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Directive:** The Character Generation Agent must create full profiles for all NPCs listed. Tier 1 NPCs are unique individuals. Tier 2 & 3 are archetypes to be instantiated multiple times with unique details as needed by the simulation.
*   **Tier 1 (Unique, Major NPCs):**
    *   **Captain Blackthorn "One-Eye" Reyes:** The ruthless pirate lord and main antagonist, a cunning strategist with a personal grudge against the Pirate King's legacy.
    *   **Navigator "Starfinder" Elara Voss:** The scholarly navigator with expertise in ancient maps and hidden knowledge about the treasure.
    *   **Boatswain "Ironfist" Marcus:** The loyal first mate whose connection to the Pirate King's final days makes him invaluable to the crew.
*   **Tier 2 & 3 (Archetypes for Generation):**
    *   "Rival Pirate Captain"
    *   "Shady Port Merchant"
    *   "Mysterious Island Native"
    *   "Treasure Map Dealer"
    *   "Sea Monster Hunter"
    *   "Cursed Treasure Guardian"

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Directive:** The Environment Generation Agent must create a detailed blueprint for each location below, populating all specified sub-sections.
*   **Location Roster:**
    *   **The Sapphire Archipelago:** A vast chain of islands forming the campaign's main setting.
        *   **Key Landmarks:** The Central Lagoon, the Ancient Observatory, the Sunken Fleet, the Treasure Map Islands.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** Various pirate crews, sea monsters, mysterious island natives, merchant vessels.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** Ship repairs, treasure maps, exotic supplies, information brokerage.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** A sudden storm reveals a hidden island, a sea monster pod migrates through the area, rival pirates attempt an ambush, discovery of a floating treasure cache, a merchant convoy requests escort.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "The archipelago's islands move with the tides." "The Pirate King's ghost still sails these waters." "The treasure is guarded by something worse than curses."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (Crystal clear waters, colorful coral formations), Sound (Waves crashing, wind in sails, distant thunder), Smell (Salt air, tropical flowers, fresh rain).
    *   **The Cursed Atoll:** A fog-shrouded ring of islands where supernatural phenomena occur.
        *   **Key Landmarks:** The Ghost Light, the Whispering Caves, the Cursed Reef, the Central Altar.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** Spectral pirates, cursed creatures, the occasional brave explorer.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** Cursed artifacts, spectral guidance, forbidden knowledge.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** A ghost ship appears in the fog, a crew member becomes possessed, discovery of a cursed treasure piece, a spectral pirate demands a test, the fog reveals hidden truths about the crew.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "The atoll is where the Pirate King died and cursed the treasure." "The fog shows you your heart's desire before killing you." "Breaking the curse requires a sacrifice."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (Thick fog, ghostly lights, distorted reflections), Sound (Distant bells, whispering voices, echoing laughter), Smell (Brine, decay, ozone).
    *   **Port Royal:** A bustling pirate haven and information hub.
        *   **Key Landmarks:** The Black Dog Tavern, the Shipyards, the Black Market, the Gallows Point.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** Pirates of all types, merchants, information brokers, entertainers.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** Ship upgrades, crew recruitment, treasure maps, entertainment.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** A bar fight erupts over treasure information, a merchant offers a shady deal, discovery of a wanted poster with the players' faces, a fellow pirate shares a crucial clue, a press gang attempts recruitment.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "Port Royal knows every secret in the archipelago." "The Pirate King's greatest treasure map is hidden here." "The port itself is built on a sunken treasure galleon."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (Crowded docks, colorful pirate flags), Sound (Loud laughter, sea shanties, haggling merchants), Smell (Rum, spices, tar).

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **Directive:** The World Simulation Agent must implement the following trigger-based state changes. For each "IF" condition, the agent must pre-generate the narrative and environmental consequences for the "THEN" outcome.
*   **Triggers:**
    *   **IF:** The players successfully acquire a major treasure map piece.
    *   **THEN:** Rival crews become more aggressive, leading to increased encounters. Generate opportunities for alliances with less hostile pirate factions.
    *   **IF:** The players defeat a rival pirate crew in a significant naval battle.
    *   **THEN:** Their reputation grows, attracting better crew members. Generate retaliation from the defeated crew's allies.
    *   **IF:** The players ally with the Free Brothers.
    *   **THEN:** They gain access to hidden ports and supply networks. Generate scenarios where the alliance is tested by moral dilemmas.
    *   **IF:** The players explore the Cursed Atoll and survive.
    *   **THEN:** They gain supernatural insights but attract the attention of guardian spirits. Generate visions that reveal personal truths about the crew.
    *   **IF:** The players discover the treasure's true nature before the final confrontation.
    *   **THEN:** They gain a significant advantage but must deal with the moral implications. Generate a scenario where the pirate lord attempts to steal this knowledge.
