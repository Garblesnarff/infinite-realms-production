**Gears & Grimoires: The Cogwheel Conspiracy**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Directive:** This is the foundational context. All generated content must align with this core premise.
*   **Content:** In the sprawling industrial city of Cogwheel Harbor, magic and machinery exist in an uneasy alliance, where enchanted automatons labor alongside human workers and spell-powered factories churn out wonders of magitech engineering. When a series of "accidents" begins befalling prominent citizens, the players discover a conspiracy to replace free will with mechanical obedience through a dangerous new form of mind-controlling magitech. As investigators, whistleblowers, or concerned citizens, they must navigate the city's labyrinthine politics, corporate espionage, and revolutionary undercurrents to expose the conspiracy. But as they delve deeper into the mystery, they discover that the real threat isn't just controlâ€”it's the fundamental question of what it means to be human in a world where magic and machines are becoming indistinguishable.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Directive:** The Lore Generation Agent must create detailed entries for each of the following prompts. These entries will form the historical and cultural bedrock of the world.
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the founding of Cogwheel Harbor and the discovery of magitech - the fusion of mechanical engineering and magical principles.
    *   Write the history of the Great Industrial Revolution that transformed the city from a small port town to an industrial powerhouse.
    *   Describe the development of magitech mind-control technology and the ethical debates surrounding human augmentation.
    *   Explain the social divide between the corporate elite in their spires and the workers in the factory districts.
    *   Detail the various worker uprisings and revolutionary movements that have shaped the city's political landscape.
    *   Write about the "Augmented Rights" movement and the fight for recognition of mechanically and magically enhanced individuals.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Directive:** The Faction Generation Agent must create a detailed profile for each faction listed below. Each profile must contain the specified fields.
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **The Cogwheel Corporation** (Major)
        *   **Goals:** To advance magitech progress at any cost, believing that human weakness is the only barrier to perfection.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Corporate board led by executives, with Professor Voss as head researcher and Lady Vale as operations director.
        *   **Public Agenda:** We bring progress and prosperity to all through the miracle of magitech engineering.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** To implement widespread mind control for the "greater good" of an ordered society.
        *   **Assets:** Vast research facilities, corporate enforcers, mind-control prototypes.
        *   **Relationships:** Antagonistic toward workers; patronizing toward the revolutionary elements.
    *   **The Ironworkers Union** (Major)
        *   **Goals:** To protect workers' rights and prevent the replacement of human labor with magitech automatons.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Union council led by Marcus "Sparks" Rodriguez and other shop floor representatives.
        *   **Public Agenda:** Workers deserve fair wages, safe conditions, and protection from dangerous magitech.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** To seize control of magitech production for the benefit of the workers.
        *   **Assets:** Strike capabilities, underground network, insider information.
        *   **Relationships:** Hostile to the Corporation; willing to ally with players who prove their commitment to workers' rights.
    *   **The Augmented Citizens League** (Minor)
        *   **Goals:** To advocate for the rights and recognition of individuals who have undergone magitech augmentation.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Council of augmented individuals from various social classes.
        *   **Public Agenda:** Augmentation is the next step in human evolution and should be celebrated, not feared.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** To ensure that augmentation technology remains accessible to all, not just the elite.
        *   **Assets:** Legal expertise, augmented abilities, network of sympathetic medical professionals.
        *   **Relationships:** Cautiously neutral toward both Corporation and Union; may ally with players depending on their stance on augmentation.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Directive:** The Character Generation Agent must create full profiles for all NPCs listed. Tier 1 NPCs are unique individuals. Tier 2 & 3 are archetypes to be instantiated multiple times with unique details as needed by the simulation.
*   **Tier 1 (Unique, Major NPCs):**
    *   **Professor Elara Voss:** The brilliant but unstable lead researcher, augmented with experimental implants that enhance her intellect but affect her sanity.
    *   **Marcus "Sparks" Rodriguez:** The charismatic revolutionary leader with a personal grudge against the conspiracy.
    *   **Lady Cassandra Vale:** The conflicted corporate executive torn between ambition and conscience.
*   **Tier 2 & 3 (Archetypes for Generation):**
    *   "Corporate Enforcer"
    *   "Factory Worker"
    *   "Augmented Citizen"
    *   "Revolutionary Spy"
    *   "Corrupt Official"
    *   "Ethical Researcher"

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Directive:** The Environment Generation Agent must create a detailed blueprint for each location below, populating all specified sub-sections.
*   **Location Roster:**
    *   **The Grand Factory Complex:** A massive industrial facility where magic and machinery work in tandem.
        *   **Key Landmarks:** The Assembly Line, the Testing Chamber, the Worker Barracks, the Quality Control Station.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** Factory workers, overseers, automated machinery, the occasional augmented supervisor.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** Basic tools, black market augmentation parts, insider information.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** A machine malfunctions spectacularly, a worker sabotage attempt, discovery of a hidden prototype, an industrial accident that requires rescue, a union meeting that turns violent.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "The assembly line never stops, even for accidents." "Some workers volunteer for experimental procedures." "The factory has a secret night shift for special projects."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (Spars flying, glowing magical runes on machinery), Sound (Hissing steam, clanking metal, shouted orders), Smell (Hot metal, ozone from magic, machine oil).
    *   **The Heart of Innovation:** A secret research facility hidden beneath the city.
        *   **Key Landmarks:** The Neural Link Chamber, the Prototype Testing Area, the Data Archives, the Containment Cells.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** Research scientists, test subjects, security automatons, Professor Voss.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** Experimental technology, research data, mind-control devices.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** A test subject breaks containment, a prototype activates unexpectedly, discovery of classified research files, an ethical researcher seeks help, a security sweep forces hiding.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "The Heart holds the key to true human evolution." "Some test subjects never return from the neural chamber." "The research began with good intentions."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (Glowing data screens, humming magical equipment), Sound (Electronic hums, distant screams, bubbling containment fluid), Smell (Sterile chemicals, electrical burning).
    *   **The Corporate Spire:** A towering skyscraper of steel and spellwork.
        *   **Key Landmarks:** The Executive Boardroom, the Penthouse Offices, the Security Center, the Public Relations Department.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** Corporate executives, PR specialists, security personnel, Lady Vale.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** Corporate contracts, luxury accommodations, political connections.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** A executive meeting reveals conspiracy details, a PR event provides cover for investigation, discovery of falsified documents, a security patrol requires fast talking, a social event offers networking opportunities.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "The Spire's upper floors are only accessible by invitation." "Executives get the best augmentations first." "The corporation has plans that would change the city forever."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (Polished marble, crystal chandeliers, holographic displays), Sound (Quiet conversations, elevator chimes, distant city noise), Smell (Expensive colognes, fresh flowers, polished brass).

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **Directive:** The World Simulation Agent must implement the following trigger-based state changes. For each "IF" condition, the agent must pre-generate the narrative and environmental consequences for the "THEN" outcome.
*   **Triggers:**
    *   **IF:** The players successfully expose a major conspiracy operation.
    *   **THEN:** Worker morale improves, leading to better intelligence and allies. Generate increased corporate crackdowns that create new missions.
    *   **IF:** The players acquire and use magitech augmentations.
    *   **THEN:** They gain enhanced abilities but attract corporate attention. Generate personal side effects that create roleplaying opportunities.
    *   **IF:** The players ally with the Ironworkers Union.
    *   **THEN:** They gain access to worker networks and strike capabilities. Generate scenarios where union conflicts create complications.
    *   **IF:** The players confront Professor Elara Voss before gathering sufficient evidence.
    *   **THEN:** She becomes a recurring antagonist with enhanced abilities. Generate opportunities to turn her to the players' side through ethical appeals.
    *   **IF:** The players choose to destroy rather than control dangerous magitech.
    *   **THEN:** The corporation suffers a major setback but becomes more desperate. Generate underground markets where scavenged magitech appears.
