# Strategic Research Priorities & Action Plan

**Created**: December 2024  
**Purpose**: Prioritized roadmap for gathering critical business intelligence  
**Timeline**: 30/90/180-day research sprint plan

## Executive Summary

To build Infinite Realms into the "YouTube of AI-Generated Entertainment," we need systematic intelligence gathering across 6 critical domains. This document prioritizes research based on **risk reduction** and **revenue acceleration** potential.

## 30-Day Sprint (Critical Path Research)

### Week 1: User Validation Blitz
**Objective**: Validate product-market fit and pricing assumptions

#### Primary Research:
- **User Interview Sprint**: 50 interviews with target solo D&D players
  - 20 rural/isolated players
  - 15 busy professionals
  - 10 social anxiety/new players
  - 5 international players

#### Key Questions:
1. How much would you pay for unlimited solo D&D?
2. What's most frustrating about finding D&D groups?
3. Have you tried AI chatbots for gaming? What was missing?
4. Would you pay for AI-generated campaign content?
5. Interest in sharing campaigns with others?

#### Deliverables:
- User persona refinement with payment willingness
- Pricing strategy validation or adjustment
- Feature priority ranking from user feedback
- Product positioning messaging validation

### Week 2: Technical Cost Modeling
**Objective**: Understand true unit economics at scale

#### Research Tasks:
- **API Cost Stress Testing**: Simulate 1k, 10k, 100k users
- **Optimization Experiments**: Test caching, batch processing savings
- **Alternative Provider Analysis**: OpenAI, Anthropic pricing comparison
- **Infrastructure Scaling**: Database and server cost projections

#### Key Metrics:
- Cost per user at different engagement levels
- Optimization potential (% cost reduction achievable)
- Break-even user count per subscription tier
- Infrastructure investment requirements

#### Deliverables:
- Updated unit economics model with optimization
- Revised pricing tiers based on actual costs
- Technical scaling roadmap with cost projections
- API provider recommendation with rationale

### Week 3: MVP Traction Analysis
**Objective**: Measure current product performance and user behavior

#### Analytics Deep Dive:
- **User Engagement Patterns**: Session length, return frequency
- **Feature Usage**: Which AI features drive retention
- **Churn Analysis**: When and why users leave
- **Content Quality**: User satisfaction with AI-generated content

#### A/B Testing:
- **Pricing experiments**: Test different subscription tiers
- **Feature testing**: AI voice vs text-only engagement
- **Onboarding optimization**: Reduce time-to-value

#### Deliverables:
- Product performance dashboard
- Retention cohort analysis
- Feature impact ranking
- Onboarding conversion optimization

### Week 4: Competitive Positioning Research
**Objective**: Refine market positioning and identify threats/opportunities

#### Intelligence Gathering:
- **AI Gaming Startup Monitoring**: Track emerging competitors
- **VTT AI Feature Analysis**: Monitor Roll20, D&D Beyond AI additions
- **Creator Economy Benchmarks**: Roblox, Minecraft, Twitch creator metrics
- **Solo Gaming Market Analysis**: Adjacent markets and solutions

#### Strategic Analysis:
- **Competitive advantages audit**: Sustainable moat identification
- **Threat assessment**: Big tech entry scenarios
- **Partnership opportunities**: Complementary platform identification
- **Market timing analysis**: Window of opportunity assessment

#### Deliverables:
- Competitive threat assessment with mitigation strategies
- Partnership opportunity pipeline
- Market positioning refinement
- Go-to-market strategy updates

## 90-Day Research Program (High Impact Initiatives)

### Month 2: Creator Economy Strategy
**Objective**: Design sustainable creator monetization and retention

#### Creator Research:
- **Creator Interviews**: 25 interviews with potential content creators
  - Independent authors/writers
  - TTRPG content creators
  - Digital artists
  - Comic creators/illustrators

#### Market Analysis:
- **Creator Platform Benchmarks**: Revenue sharing models that work
- **Content Quality Standards**: Community moderation approaches
- **Creator Tools Requirements**: What tools drive creator success
- **Revenue Diversification**: Multiple income stream strategies

#### Deliverables:
- Creator onboarding and retention strategy
- Revenue sharing model recommendations
- Creator tools development roadmap
- Community guidelines and moderation framework

### Month 3: Financial Modeling & Funding
**Objective**: Develop comprehensive financial plan and funding strategy

#### Financial Model Development:
- **5-year revenue projections** across all phases
- **Customer acquisition cost modeling** by channel
- **Lifetime value analysis** with retention curves
- **Funding requirements** by phase and milestone

#### Market Validation:
- **TAM/SAM/SOM analysis** with bottom-up validation
- **Customer acquisition testing** across marketing channels
- **International market sizing** for expansion planning
- **Enterprise opportunity assessment** for B2B revenue

#### Deliverables:
- Complete financial model with scenario planning
- Funding strategy with investor targeting
- Customer acquisition playbook
- International expansion feasibility study
